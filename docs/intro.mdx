### What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence with physical systems. Unlike traditional AI that operates purely in digital spaces, Physical AI encompasses systems that interact with, understand, and manipulate the physical world. This includes:

- **Perception**: Understanding the 3D environment through vision, touch, and other sensors
- **Embodiment**: Having a physical form that interacts with the world
- **Manipulation**: The ability to act upon and change the physical environment
- **Locomotion**: Moving through and navigating physical spaces
- **Interaction**: Engaging with humans and other physical entities

### The Rise of Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robots represent the pinnacle of physical AI integration. These systems combine:

- **Biological Inspiration**: Human-like form and movement patterns
- **Advanced AI**: Sophisticated decision-making and learning capabilities
- **Complex Control Systems**: Real-time coordination of multiple subsystems
- **Human-Robot Interaction**: Natural communication and collaboration
